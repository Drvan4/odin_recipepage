Ingredients

  • 1 part Aperol
  • 1 part Prosecco
  • A splash of club soda
  • 1 slice of fresh orange

Instructions

  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Add Aperol
  3. Add Prosecco
  4. Add club soda
  5. Finish with a slice of orange
Nice on a hot day!
